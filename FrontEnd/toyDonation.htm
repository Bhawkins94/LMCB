The Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau accepts new toys, that re not gift-wrapped, gift items and cash donatoins. There is a larger need for gifts for tweens and teens. We try to limit the number of stuffed toys received as our warehouse is already overflowing with their cuteness.

We accept donations all year round. Please call or email the office to book an appointment.

T:(604) 253-7191
E:office@lmcb.ca

Christmas Wishes
We want to ensure that we give gifts with the best play value while suiting the needs of the recipients the most. Oftentimes we run short of toys for tweens and teens. They are still kids to us and we always do our best to be there for them. Please consider our Christmas Wishlist.

Babies, Toddlers & Preschoolers
Outdoor toys (tricycle, kick scooter, balance bike), building blocks (Megablocks, wooden blocks), Play-Doh, musical toys (xylophone, maracas, drums), educational toys (Leapfrog, Co-operative board games, Trace-n-Erase chalkboards, Magna-Tiles, Toy Toolbox), crayons, paints & easels, age appropriate electronics, toy cars and train sets, magnetic fishing sets, and dolls.

Elementary School Kids
Building toys (Lego, Duplo), outdoor toys (skateboard, kick scooter, bike, soccer ball, basketball, sled), games & brain twisters (Pokemon cards, Perplexus, Monopoly, Cranium Family Edition, Sorry, Twister, Chutes and Ladders, Hedbanz, Pictopia, Ant Colony, Jenga), Barbie, arts and craft sets (pencils, crayons, pastels, DIY sets, slime), backpacks, Nerf, remote control cars, racetracks, miniature golf kit, science lab kits, smart pixelator, Pictionary Air, jewelry making sets, and kids coding games.

Tweens & Teens
Electronics, headphones, wireless earbuds, portable chargers, Fujifilm Instax or Polaroid Camera and film, musical instruments (guitar, keyboard, etc), bluetooth speakers, tablets, VR Headset, watch/smartwatches, fitness/fruit infusion water bottles, Mini Cinema Lightbox, Scratch off posters, video game consoles, books, backpacks, tea & coffee travel mug, make-up, jewelry, curling wands and hair straighteners, Nerf toys, and boardgames (Catan, Cards Against Humanity, Scrabble, Ticket to Ride, Codenames, Risk, Exploding Kittens, Munchkins, Pandemic).

Tickets to concerts & sports events and gift cards are great so that tweens and teens can pick out something for themselves that they will love and need (coffee, mall, store).
