Create a lasting legacy with the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau by leaving a gift in your will


Your planned gift to the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau can create a lasting legacy that empowers families in need to create a lasting legacy that empowers families in need to create memorable Christmas experiences for their kids.


Planned giving arrangements can:

  • Continue your support for the LMCB
  • Create a legacy that reflects your values
  • Opportunity to give more through your will than you may be able to give today
  • Make a valuable commitment without impacting your finances today
  • Reduce your estate taxes
  • Provide flexibility in changing your gift intentions over time


Wording for your will to leave a bequest:

Sample bequest wording:

Specific Bequest: "I give to the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau, charitable registration number 12978 7487 RR0001, the sum of $______."

Residual Bequest: "I give to the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau, charitable registration number 12978 7487 RR0001, ______% of the residue of my estate."

Contact the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau at (604) 253-7191 or office@lmcb.ca to learn more about how you can make a legacy donation.

Thank you for your meaningful consideration.
