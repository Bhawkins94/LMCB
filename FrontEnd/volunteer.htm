Christmas Toy Store Volunteer
Seasonal December
We are looking for adult volunteers to assist in our Toy Store for our Vancouver Christmas Giving Program.

Volunteers are needed from December 4th to December 20th, Monday-Fridays.

Volunteers will assist parents with choosing gifts for their children in a one-on-one setting as they shop in our free Christmas Toy Store.

Gift Sorting in LMCB's Warehouse
Monday to Friday shifts
The Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau collects, sorts and ships 75,000 to 100,000 items every year. This takes a huge effort by many volunteers. If you have even a few hours to spare we welcome your support.
