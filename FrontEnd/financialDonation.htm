Thank you for joining our community of supporters that began in 1930, almost 90 years ago! Your support of the Lower Mainland Christmas Bureau will help to create a meaningful impact in empowering families in need to create Christmas experiences for their kids through the generosity of community.


Your support makes a wonderful difference


Last year, because of the generous contributions from our supporters, the LMCB helped close to 1,000 low-income families in Vancouver and provided gifts to almost 2,000 kids. In additon, the kindness of all the monetary and toy donations enabled us to distribute over 100,000 toys to bureaus and agencies across the Lower Mainland.
